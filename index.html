ABOUT

Grace Hospital

Grace Hospital is a health care organization currently residing at 7, Jireh street, Salem. It is a member of the Salvation Ark Medical Association(SAMA) which is an international association that comprises of outstanding healthcare organizations across the world.
It was established in the year 2003 by Apostle David Adebanjo. Apostle David Adebanjo is a renowned cardiologist who bags a PhD from the University of Reading, UK. Click here to know more about him.
